Hot Dog

Description

It's an American food that is easily made using a bun of bread and a large meat called sausages. It may seen simple and it is but can also add lots of flavor thanks to how easy is to simply add the new ingredients over the food.

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Bun bread (Hot Dog bread)
  • Sausages
  • Anything you want to add

Steps

  1. First you must make a cut slice in one of the bread's sides.
  2. Add the mayonnaise inside of the cut slice made in the bread.
  3. Add the sausage in the cut slice, and done.
  4. You can add more flavor over the sausage or even between the sausage and the bread.
