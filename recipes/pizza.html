Pizza

Description

It's a italian food served that consist of a big bread covered with a mixture of ingredients (anything you want) and sauces that is served in a big circular dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon og sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of vegetal oil
  • Salt
  • Water
  • 100 ml tomato sauce
  • Any vegetables
  • Any meat
  • 100 gm of any cheese type

Steps

  1. To prepare the Pizza dough, add the all purpose flour and start mixing it up with salt and baking powder then make sure to spread everything out well around the center and corners. Add the some warm water, yeast plus the teaspoon of sugar and vegetal oil in the mixture. Keep mixing up the dough for 10 to 15 minutes. When the yeast rises add the flour knead the dough nicely using water and keep this dough aside for 4 to 6 hours. Then knead the dough once again and the pizza dough is ready.
  2. Preheat the oven at 180 degree Celsius, make the pizza base with the dough made previously. Dust the space with little dry flour and grab a large amount of the pizza dough and using a rolling pin, roll this dough into a nice circular base, once the base it's made use a fork and prick and put the pizza base in the preheated oven and bake it for 10 minutes.
  3. Now slice your vegetables and meat so we can prepare them for the pizza, once the pizza base is baked add some tomato sauce and spread it around and add all the vegetables and meat you want with a large amount of cheese of your choice and then we put the pizza on the oven once again but make sure the oven is 250 degree Celsius and bake it for 10 minutes and your done.
